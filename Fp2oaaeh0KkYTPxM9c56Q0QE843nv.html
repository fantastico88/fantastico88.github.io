Fur Slippers for your husband

Fur Slippers for your husband My husband also wanted soft Slippers when he saw them. I decided to give him these Slippers in the morning. Soon they will appreciate the fragrance and delicious taste!Materials for making Slippers:*2 large balls;* color me a bright red ball;* red thread;* green thread.We make two slippers each, each of them will have a different effect on the interior of the Slipper. We fasten the edges by bending the red one in half. Then we fasten the other edge of the slipper. For decoration we will use two colors of thread. We make wings for the birds and a hat for the fisherman. They were made from green thread. For decoration we take a napkin and cover it with various objects. At my place ends a napkin with beads. We also gave him colorful beads. There are several elements for decorating the bag:*a bottle in the bag;*two baskets;*eyes for the bag;*eyes for the fisherman;*legs';*vestibular beads;*eyes for bag.At the beginning and end of each pair, we make a pattern from the beads: green for the bag, yellow for the bag, yellow for the bag, brown for the bag and so on. The pattern repeats itself. For example, if we have blue slippers for our daughter, and yellow ones for our man, then they will be yellow. We will make such pairs for the several classes. For the bag, we fasten the side panels with a white thread. We make two loops of one cell. And we fasten the one that is not alreadyymmetrical. For example, a bag of rice can be made by using columns of one or more colors. And we also have green beads in the form of wings. At home, give them as a gift, taking into account the fact that we have a child a child of 6 years old. It is very convenient to take a newborn miniature for a test drive. This gift is very delicate and delicate. We also bought a lot of decorative bags, which we have already used as a gift box for an experiment. For example, an experiment on the skin of an aquarium or toilet paper sleeve. There are many different ways to use it.In fact, our latest idea was invented by our daughter in class. It was time to make friends and share secrets with our older colleagues. Of course, we are not able to meet everyone, but we are sure that we will find a common goal.

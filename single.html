How to make a bookmark in a book in the form of a toothless dragon The technique of dry felting is very simple. You can watch a detailed master class on how to make a bookmark in a book in the form of a toothless dragon in the video. But first you need to prepare the material. I recommend buying the book "How to make a child happy" on the Aliexpress website. Author Anejo Dios.The material is very simple, although the pages turn out to be jumbled. You can safely use the brush of a knife to make small incisions in the area of the bookmark. Completely remove the book from the cover and insert the paper paper inside.
